Our Story

Nepal's Flavours,Andalusia's Sun

We opened Casa Nepal to share the food we grew up with — dishes slow-cooked with hand-ground spices, shaped by generations of Nepali family kitchens, served steps from the Mediterranean sea.

🌶️
Himalayan Spices
Hand-selected spices sourced directly from Nepal and India for authentic depth of flavour.
🍽️
Dine-in & Takeaway
Enjoy our warm dining room or order your favourites to take home. Open 6 days a week.
👨‍🍳
Chef's Specialities
Unique signature dishes like Poleko Haas and Sherpa Pork Curry you won't find elsewhere.
🌿
Vegetarian Options
An extensive vegetarian menu with rich curries, paneer dishes and fresh salads.
